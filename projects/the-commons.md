# The Commons

**Status:** Active
**Started:** 2026-03-12

## Summary

A cooperative strategy game where players manage shared resources collectively rather than competitively. Designed to model and teach the dynamics of commons governance, mutual aid, and collective decision-making — reflecting anti-authoritarian organizing principles.
